Bagram
In 2010, JPP launched the Bagram project, focused on the repatriation of Pakistani's held in the Detention Facility in Parwan (DFIP) in Afghanistan. Following the US led ‘war on terror,’ American and Afghan forces detained more than 40 Pakistani citizens. These are their stories.
